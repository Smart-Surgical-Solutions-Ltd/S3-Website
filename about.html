S3

We are a London-based enterprise with roots at Imperial College London, where our journey began with a mission to revolutionise surgical navigation platform technology. Our focus is on developing innovative, cost-effective solutions to enhance the accuracy and accessibility of Total Knee Replacement (TKR) procedures. By combining advanced technology with user-friendly design, we aim to empower surgeons worldwide, enhancing patient outcomes while reducing costs and complexity in orthopedic surgery—especially in areas where expensive robotic systems are inaccessible or impractical.
